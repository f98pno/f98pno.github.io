About Respite

The Respite fit-testing technology was developed as a result of the urgent mask shortage that took place during the COVID-19 pandemic. We heard stories of huge shipments of masks not fitting well enough to protect frontline workers. As the crisis accelerated, we could no longer sit back and watch our healthcare workers suffer without protective supplies.


We met through the Antler program and decided to put our heads together to solve the issue of providing the best fitting mask models when traditional fit-testing is not a possibility. So far, there is no other existing technology to guarantee the right fit of respirator masks. RESPITE fills this critical gap in the market.

Our vision

We aim to protect public health and frontline medical workers through innovative technologies.

Our mission

We ensure that frontline medical workers are guaranteed fast access to lifesaving respirator masks during an epidemic or pandemic through our ML-powered qualitative fit-testing method.
